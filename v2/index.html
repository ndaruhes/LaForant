We Are LaForant

Focus On Your Goal

Two armed groups of different backgrounds and thoughts are taken to an isolated place by a mysterious organization called 'Lafor'. They were told there were only 10 rounds in one game. Every one round, all groups will come back to life. One group has to think fast or they will be killed by the opponent. The biggest points will be won and may leave the game. Take on foes across Competitive and Unranked modes as well as Deathmatch. Feel a new style and experience in playing FPS games.
